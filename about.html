About

Hi, I'm Vivek — a data engineer by profession, a systems thinker by habit, and someone who occasionally goes down rabbit holes on how things *actually* work.

I currently work in the adtech measurement space, building data platforms that make sense of messy, multi-channel campaign data (think APIs, pipelines, and a lot of “why does this metric not match?” moments). My work sits at the intersection of distributed systems, data modeling, and analytics.

Before this, I completed my Master's in Computer Engineering (AI/ML) from the University of Waterloo, where I also worked on zero-effort health tech at UbiLab.

Prior to grad school, I spent a few years in the industry — as a Data Engineer at ZS Associates working on data warehousing and BI for large pharmaceutical clients, and earlier at Airtel, where I built data platforms from scratch and worked on real-time systems to improve visibility into streaming network metrics in the telecom domain.

Outside of work, I'm curious about distributed systems, real-time data, ML systems, and the occasional deep dive into networks, IoT, or “why AI isn’t as magical as it sounds.”

This blog is where I think out loud.
You'll find posts on data systems, adtech, measurement, and the weird edge cases that don’t show up in clean diagrams. Some are lessons learned, some are breakdowns of complex ideas, and some are just me trying to make sense of things by writing them down.

Expect fewer buzzwords, more clarity, and the occasional opinion.
